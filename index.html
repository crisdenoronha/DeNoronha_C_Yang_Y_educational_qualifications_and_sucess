A discussion about Educational Qualifications

Topic:Some people believe that educational qualifications will always bring success in life. Other people say that educational qualifications do not necessarily bring success. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

Educational Qualifications

Written by Ying Yang

A number of people hold a view that educational qualifications is a prerequisite for being successful, while others think that educational certifications are not the necessary conditions for getting success. In my opinion, I think people should get educational qualifications which help them become successful men quickly.

Firstly, success is based on real-life and they need money to achieve it. If they want to earn money, they need to find a job that provides basic needs for themselves. However, if they do not have an educational qualification, they cannot find a position in job markets. The reason is that educational qualification is a prerequisite for a job finder. For example, if a male wants to be an accountant, he needs to get a certification from a college. If a female hopes to find a teaching position, she should hold a bachelor's degree from a university. All in all, when people cannot demonstrate that they have educational qualifications in the interview processing, their resume will be directly thrown into the trash can.
